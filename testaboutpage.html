PureFoods - About

Know more about us!

At Pure Foods, we strive to sell products of the highest quality at a low cost!
Our goal is to ensure that inclusivity is met by showcasing a wide variety of products to appease customers of all types of backgrounds.

We ensure that the process of buying products is as simple and efficient as possible.
We also ensure that are employees work to the best of their ability and at the same time we uphold their rights and develop a safe, inclusive workplace.

Our history

Since 1869, we have been helping to give customers a wide range of high quality products.
Our comapny started as a humble little shop in Birmingham offering hand made products to passers-by.
Since then, our company has expanded to over 200 stores across the UK as well as the website you are in right now!

Climate action

Considering the big issues regarding the climate, one of our main goals is to ensure sustainable shopping to help tackle the global crisis
which includes helping to reduce carbon emissions and discouraging the use of plastic bags.
One of a priorities is to improve both customer experiences and the environment!
